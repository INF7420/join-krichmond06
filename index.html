About Katie

I love learning.
Knowledge excites me. I live to hear new stories and meet new people. That's why I decided on a career in libray science. In the meantime, I am working as a teen librarian and curriculation assistant at a small public library near Madison, WI. I have jumped headlong into the library science field and am loving every second of it!
