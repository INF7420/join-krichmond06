My Work

Library

Oregon Public Library

January 2018-August 2018

I began my career in library science as a page at Oregon Public Library. I loved being an integral part of curriculation and challenging myself to perform quickly and accurately. My responsiblities included:

  • Shelving books, processing holds, and shelf reading
  • Assisting to open and close the library
  • Creating wall displays for the children's area and assisting the children's librarian with various tasks

Edgerton Public Library

May 2018-Present

I accepted a summer curriculation assistant position at Edgerton Public Library that morphed into a permanent position as an assistant and teen services librarian in the fall of 2018. My responsiblities include:

  • Planning and implementing teen programs
  • Creating relevant and engaging book displays for the teen population
  • Delivering one-on-one technology assistance to patrons of all ages
  • Working the curriculation desk and processing holds

National Service

I have always been drawn to community service, and spent two years utilizing my time and skills to serve others through AmeriCorps.

AmeriCorps VISTA Homeless Student Coordinator

August 2016-August 2017

As an AmeriCorps VISTA Homeless Student Coordinator, I performed the following tasks:

  • Remotely supported 35 school district homeless liaisons in implementation of the McKinney-Vento Homeless Assistance Act
  • Created online community resource guides and resource binders
  • Organized and hosted a homeless liaison network meeting

Minnesota Reading Corps K-3 Literacy Tutor

August 2015-July 2016

As a Minnesota Reading Corps K-3 Literacy Tutor, I performed the following tasks:

  • Deliverd one-on-one literacy interventions to 27 students in grades K-3
  • Performed daily data entry and administered weekly progress monitoring tests
  • Partnered with St. Paul Public Schools Community Education to serve as a Lead Teacher/Planner for two week-long summer camps for students in grades 1-5 and 6-8
